Travelogue: Paris

Overview

The capital of France is known for its extensive history, food, art, and fashion! With its iconlic landmarks, great atmosphere, and beautiful streets, it's unlikely you'll ever get bored! Whether it's just a trip with family/friends, or the next place you're thinking to live, Paris will always be a great choice!

Must-See Spots

  1. Eiffel Tower
  2. Louvre Museum
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral
  4. Champs-Élysées
  5. Seine River Cruise

Visitor Tips

  • If it's within your budget, buy museum tickets to avoid long lines.
  • Use the Metro to travel. It's quick and affordable!
  • Learn some French phrases. Doesn't have to be anything difficult!
  • Bring shoes you'll be comfortable in for long walks.
